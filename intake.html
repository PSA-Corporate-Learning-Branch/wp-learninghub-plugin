What Gets Included in the LearningHUB?

LearningHUB courses are formal learning opportunities that are available to all BC Public employees, no matter what ministry you're in, or what level you are.

As a BCPS employee, you will find all the courses within available and open for registration. Courses that are no longer offered are regularily removed from the catalog.

Is the course formal learning?

Formal Learning is a composed set of resources designed to meet an end-goal, generally facilitated and evaluated in some way, resulting in a verifiable credit via an institution or organization.

The LearningHUB catalog is a collaborative effort between numerous learning Partners, with representatives from each partner responsible for the courses listed under their banner.

If you represent an existing Learning Partner as found on the partner page, then we definitely encourage you to add your course to the LearningHUB! If you're not the primary contact for your org, we might be able to help you find them.

Is the course informal learning?

Informal learning are resources which may be invaluable by themselves, but your consumption of the content is not tracked or verifiable by an institution or organization as part of a structured approach to a particular topic.

By themselves, informal learning opportunities cannot be included into the LearningHUB without additional structure and context, hopefully adding to an existing formal experience, or by creating an entirely new one.

Can the course be formalized?

Learning Curator Pathways offer a great avenue to add formalization to disparate resources. Check out the available pathways to see where this content might fit best!
